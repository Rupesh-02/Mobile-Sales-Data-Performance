📱 Mobile Sales Dashboard - Power BI Project


📌 Project Overview:

This project is a Power BI dashboard developed to analyze mobile sales data and generate business insights from different 
brands, cities, customers, and payment methods.

The dashboard helps in understanding:

  ● Sales performance of different mobile brands
  
  ● Customer purchasing behavior
  
  ● City-wise sales analysis
  
  ● Payment method trends
  
  ● Customer ratings and satisfaction
  
  ● Revenue and unit sales insights



🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

● Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation

● Microsoft Excel – Dataset storage and preprocessing

● DAX – Data calculations and measures

● Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation

  ● Units Sold
  
  ● Price Per Unit

  ● Payment Method

🎯 Key Insights

Based on the dashboard analysis:

Total Sales: 769M

Total Quantity Sold: 19K units

Total Transactions: 4K

Average Price: 40K


📌 Brand Performance

Apple generated the highest sales among all brands.

Samsung and OnePlus also contributed significantly to total revenue.

Vivo recorded comparatively lower sales than Apple and Samsung.


📌 Top Mobile Models

Galaxy Note 20 achieved the highest sales among mobile models.

Galaxy S21 and Galaxy A51 were also top-performing models.


📌 Monthly Sales Trend

Sales quantity showed strong performance during March and July.

September recorded a slight decline in quantity sold.

Overall monthly sales remained stable throughout the year.


📌 City-wise Sales Analysis

Major metropolitan cities contributed the highest sales.

Sales distribution is spread across multiple cities in India.


📌 Customer Ratings

Most customers provided high ratings.

5-star ratings were the most common, indicating positive customer satisfaction.


📌 Payment Method Analysis

Transactions are distributed across multiple payment methods.

Payment usage is relatively balanced among available methods.
